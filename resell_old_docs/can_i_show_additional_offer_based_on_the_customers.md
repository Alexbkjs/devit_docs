# Can I show additional offer based on the customers' location?

## Show Offer By Country

Yes, ReSell this option. You can show certain recommended products for certain countries. You can also restrict shipping by country.

1. Click on **Add condition** on the first step when you create a funnel
2. Shipping -> **Restrict by country**.
3. Select the country that you need.
