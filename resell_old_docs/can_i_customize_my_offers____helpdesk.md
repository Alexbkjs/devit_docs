# Can I customize my offers?

## Customization Tools

Sure! ReSell has a built-in editor. Lots of different widgets are available for customization in post-purchase and Thank you page offers. NO coding skills are required. All offers can be customized, so they will look harmonious with the design of your store.

To edit your additional offer, click on the Edit offer in the upper-right corner of the offer's block. In the opened window, you will see availbale customization tools.
