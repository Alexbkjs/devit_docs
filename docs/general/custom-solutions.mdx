At DevIT.Software, we understand that every business is unique. Our custom Shopify solutions are designed to address your specific challenges and help you achieve your e-commerce goals.

## What We Build

### Custom Shopify Apps

We develop private Shopify apps tailored to your business requirements:

- **Internal Tools**: Streamline your operations with custom admin tools
- **Customer-Facing Features**: Enhance the shopping experience with unique functionality
- **Integration Apps**: Connect your store with proprietary systems and services
- **Workflow Automation**: Automate repetitive tasks and business processes

### Theme Development & Customization

Transform your store's appearance and functionality:

- **Custom Theme Development**: Build a unique theme from scratch
- **Theme Modifications**: Enhance existing themes with custom features
- **Performance Optimization**: Speed improvements and code optimization
- **Responsive Design**: Ensure perfect display across all devices

### API Integrations

Connect your Shopify store with external systems:

- **ERP Integration**: Sync inventory, orders, and customer data
- **CRM Systems**: Integrate with Salesforce, HubSpot, and others
- **Payment Gateways**: Custom payment processing solutions
- **Shipping Providers**: Custom shipping and fulfillment integrations
- **Marketing Platforms**: Connect with email, SMS, and advertising tools

### Shopify Plus Solutions

Advanced development for enterprise merchants:

- **Custom Checkout**: Leverage checkout extensibility for unique experiences
- **B2B Features**: Wholesale portals, custom pricing, and bulk ordering
- **Multi-Store Management**: Centralized management across multiple stores
- **Advanced Automation**: Complex workflows using Shopify Flow
