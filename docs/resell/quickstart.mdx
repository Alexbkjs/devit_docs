This guide will walk you through setting up ReSell and creating your first sales funnel. You'll be ready to start increasing your average order value in less than 10 minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- An active Shopify store
- ReSell app installed from the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/resell)
- Products in your store that you want to upsell or cross-sell
- Compatible payment processor configured (see [Payment Compatibility](/resell/advanced/payment-compatibility))

## Step 1: Configure Checkout Settings

To enable post-purchase offers, you must select ReSell as your post-purchase app in Shopify settings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Shopify Settings">
    From your Shopify admin, click **Settings** in the bottom left corner
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Checkout">
    Click **Checkout** in the settings menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select ReSell">
    In the **Post-purchase page** section, select **ReSell** from the dropdown
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** at the top right to apply the settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
For detailed instructions, see [Shopify's official documentation](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/checkout-settings/checkout-style#set-post-purchase-app) on post-purchase apps.
</Note>

## Step 2: Create Your First Funnel

Now you're ready to create your first sales funnel.

### Choose Your Creation Method

ReSell offers two ways to create funnels:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="AI Template (Recommended for Beginners)">
    **AI Template** automatically creates a complete funnel with suggested products and offers.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Create Funnel">
        From the **Dashboard** or **Funnels** tab, click **Create funnel**
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select AI Template">
        Choose **AI template** option
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review Generated Funnel">
        ReSell will automatically create a funnel with smart product recommendations
      </Step>
      <Step title="Customize (Optional)">
        Edit any settings, products, or designs as needed
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish">
        Click **Publish** to activate your funnel
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
    AI templates are great for getting started quickly. You can always edit them later to fine-tune your offers.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Manual Setup (Full Control)">
    **Manual setup** gives you complete control over triggers, conditions, and product selections.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Create Funnel">
        From the **Dashboard** or **Funnels** tab, click **Create funnel**
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Manual">
        Choose **Manually** option
      </Step>
      <Step title="Follow the funnel wizard">
        Complete each step of the funnel creation process (detailed below)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Manual Funnel Configuration

If you chose manual setup, you'll go through these steps:

1. **Set Trigger**: Choose which product(s) trigger the funnel
   - Specific product
   - Product from collection
   - Any product

2. **Add Display Conditions** (Optional): Target specific customers
   - Location (country, state)
   - Cart value (minimum/maximum)
   - Customer tags

3. **Configure Offers**: Select products to upsell
   - Upsell Offer #1 (main offer)
   - Upsell Offer #2 (if first accepted)
   - Downsell Offer (if first declined)

4. **Customize Design**: Adjust colors, text, and layout

5. **Publish**: Activate your funnel

<Tip>
For detailed guidance on each step, see our [Display Conditions](/resell/features/display-conditions) and [Offer Types](/resell/features/offer-types) guides.
</Tip>

<Success>
Congratulations! Your first funnel is now live and will start displaying offers to customers.
</Success>
