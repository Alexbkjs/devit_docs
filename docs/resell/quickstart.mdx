This guide will walk you through setting up ReSell and creating your first sales funnel. You'll be ready to start increasing your average order value in less than 10 minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- An active Shopify store
- ReSell app installed from the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/resell)
- Products in your store that you want to upsell or cross-sell
- Compatible payment processor configured (see [Payment Compatibility](/resell/advanced/payment-compatibility))

## Step 1: Configure Checkout Settings

To enable post-purchase offers, you must select ReSell as your post-purchase app in Shopify settings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Shopify Settings">
    From your Shopify admin, click **Settings** in the bottom left corner
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Checkout">
    Click **Checkout** in the settings menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select ReSell">
    In the **Post-purchase page** section, select **ReSell** from the dropdown
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** at the top right to apply the settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
For detailed instructions, see [Shopify's official documentation](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/checkout-settings/checkout-style#set-post-purchase-app) on post-purchase apps.
</Note>

## Step 2: Create Your First Funnel

Now you're ready to create your first sales funnel.

### Choose Your Creation Method

ReSell offers two ways to create funnels:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="AI Template (Recommended for Beginners)">
    **AI Template** automatically creates a complete funnel with suggested products and offers.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Create Funnel">
        From the **Dashboard** or **Funnels** tab, click **Create funnel**
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select AI Template">
        Choose **AI template** option
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review Generated Funnel">
        ReSell will automatically create a funnel with smart product recommendations
      </Step>
      <Step title="Customize (Optional)">
        Edit any settings, products, or designs as needed
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish">
        Click **Publish** to activate your funnel
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
    AI templates are great for getting started quickly. You can always edit them later to fine-tune your offers.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Manual Setup (Full Control)">
    **Manual setup** gives you complete control over triggers, conditions, and product selections.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Create Funnel">
        From the **Dashboard** or **Funnels** tab, click **Create funnel**
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Manual">
        Choose **Manually** option
      </Step>
      <Step title="Follow the funnel wizard">
        Complete each step of the funnel creation process (detailed below)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Manual Funnel Configuration

If you chose manual setup, follow these detailed steps:

#### Set Display Conditions

Define when your funnel should trigger:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Certain Product">
    Trigger the funnel when a customer purchases a specific product.

    **Example**: When customer buys "iPhone 14 Pro" → Show phone case offer

    1. Select **Certain product** as trigger type
    2. Click **Choose product**
    3. Select the product from your catalog
    4. Optionally add conditions (location, cart value, etc.)
    5. Click **Next step**
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Product from Collection">
    Trigger the funnel when a customer purchases any product from a collection.

    **Example**: When customer buys from "Summer Dresses" collection → Show accessories

    1. Select **Product from collection**
    2. Choose the collection
    3. Optionally add conditions
    4. Click **Next step**
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Any Product">
    Apply the funnel to all purchases in your store.

    **Example**: Show best-selling accessory to every customer

    1. Select **Any product**
    2. Add conditions to target specific segments (optional)
    3. Click **Next step**
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

#### Configure Checkout Offers (Shopify Plus Only)

<Note>
This step appears **only for Shopify Plus** merchants. Skip if you're on a different plan.
</Note>

Select products to offer during the checkout process **before** payment:

1. Click **Add offer**
2. Choose product, collection, or auto-recommendation
3. Configure discount (optional)
4. Click **Next step**

#### Set Up Post-Purchase Offers

This is where you configure the main upsell and cross-sell offers.

**Offer Sequence**:
1. **Upsell Offer #1**: Shown first to all customers
2. If accepted → **Upsell Offer #2** appears
3. If declined → **Downsell Offer** appears instead

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upsell Offer #1">
    The primary offer shown immediately after purchase.

    1. Click **Add offer**
    2. Select offer type:
       - **Specific Product**: Choose exact product to offer
       - **Collection**: Let customer choose from collection
       - **Auto**: Use AI recommendations
    3. Configure product settings:
       - Set discount percentage or fixed amount (optional)
       - Customize product description
       - Choose product variant display
    4. Click **Save**

    **Best Practices**:
    - Offer products 30-50% of the original purchase price
    - Provide 10-20% discount to increase conversions
    - Use complementary products (accessories, add-ons)
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Upsell Offer #2">
    Secondary offer shown if customer accepts first upsell.

    1. Click **Add offer** in Upsell #2 section
    2. Choose a different product or collection
    3. Consider offering:
       - Related products
       - Bulk quantity discounts
       - Product bundles
    4. Click **Save**

    **Example Flow**:
    - Customer buys phone → Accepts case (Upsell #1) → Sees screen protector (Upsell #2)
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Downsell Offer">
    Alternative offer shown if customer declines Upsell #1.

    1. Click **Add offer** in Downsell section
    2. Choose strategy:
       - **Deeper Discount**: Same product with larger discount
       - **Alternative Product**: Lower-priced complementary item
    3. Configure offer
    4. Click **Save**

    **Best Practices**:
    - Offer 20-30% discount (higher than upsell)
    - Consider free shipping threshold
    - Use lower-priced alternatives
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
You can edit any offer by clicking **Edit offer** and customizing elements like titles, images, buttons, and layout using the drag-and-drop editor.
</Tip>

#### Configure Thank You Page Offer

Display promotional content on the order confirmation page:

1. Toggle **Enable Thank You Page Offer**
2. Add promotional content:
   - Special coupons for next purchase
   - Newsletter signup
   - Social media follow prompts
   - Related product recommendations
3. Click **Next step**

#### Set Up POS Offers (If Applicable)

If you use Shopify POS for physical retail locations:

1. Enable POS offers
2. Select products to offer at checkout terminal
3. Configure display settings
4. Click **Next step**

#### Configure Order Status Page

Show offers on the order tracking page:

1. Click **Add offer**
2. Choose products, collections, or auto-recommendations
3. These appear when customers check order status
4. Click **Next step**

#### Publish Your Funnel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Settings">
    Double-check all funnel configurations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optional Review">
    Select **"Request review from support team"** if you want our team to verify setup
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Publish">
    Activate your funnel to start showing offers
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Success>
Congratulations! Your first funnel is now live and will start displaying offers to customers.
</Success>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many funnels can I create?">
    There's no limit! Create as many funnels as you need for different products, collections, and customer segments. See [Funnel Management](/resell/features/funnel-management) for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will customers need to re-enter payment info?">
    No! Post-purchase offers use the payment information already provided during checkout, making acceptance frictionless and secure.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my payment processor isn't supported?">
    Some payment methods have limitations. Check our [Payment Compatibility](/resell/advanced/payment-compatibility) guide for the full list of supported processors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I test funnels before going live?">
    Yes! Place test orders in your store to see exactly how funnels appear to customers. Use Shopify's test mode for payment testing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
