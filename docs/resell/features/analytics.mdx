## Key Metrics Explained

### Impressions

The number of times your offers were displayed to customers.

**What it tells you**:
- How many eligible customers are seeing offers
- Whether your trigger conditions are too restrictive
- Traffic volume through your funnels

**Optimization tips**:
- Low impressions? Broaden display conditions
- High impressions but low conversions? Improve offer relevance

### Conversion Rate

Percentage of customers who accepted at least one offer.

**Calculation**: (Accepted Offers รท Total Impressions) ร 100

**Industry benchmarks**:
- **Good**: 15-20% conversion rate
- **Great**: 20-30% conversion rate
- **Excellent**: 30%+ conversion rate

**Factors affecting conversion**:
- Offer relevance to original purchase
- Discount percentage
- Product positioning
- Offer placement timing
- Design and messaging

### Revenue Generated

Total additional revenue from accepted upsells and cross-sells.

**What it includes**:
- All accepted post-purchase offers
- Checkout upsells (Shopify Plus)
- Order status page purchases

**What it excludes**:
- Original order value
- Thank you page clicks (tracked separately)

### Average Order Value (AOV) Impact

How much ReSell increases your average order value.

**Calculation**: Average order value with accepted offers vs without

**Example**:
- Original AOV: $50
- AOV with ReSell: $65
- **Impact**: +30% ($15 increase per order)

### Per-Funnel Metrics

Each funnel shows:

- **Total Impressions**: How many times this funnel was triggered
- **Accepted Offers**: Number of customers who accepted
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage who accepted
- **Revenue**: Income generated by this funnel
- **Top Performing Products**: Which upsells convert best

## Offer-Level Analytics

Drill down into individual offer performance:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Offer Views** | How many customers saw this specific offer |
| **Accept Rate** | Percentage who clicked "Add to Order" |
| **Decline Rate** | Percentage who clicked "No Thanks" |
| **Revenue per Offer** | Average revenue when offer is accepted |

Use this data to:
- Identify which products make the best upsells
- Test different discount levels
- Optimize offer sequencing (Upsell #1 vs #2 vs Downsell)

## Tracking Integration

### Facebook Pixel & Meta Ads

ReSell automatically sends events to Facebook Pixel for tracking:

**Events Tracked**:
- `ViewContent`: When offer is displayed
- `AddToCart`: When offer is accepted
- `Purchase`: When upsell payment completes

**Setup**:
1. Configure Facebook Pixel in Shopify admin
2. ReSell automatically detects and uses your pixel
3. View upsell conversions in Facebook Ads Manager

**Benefits**:
- Track ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) including upsells
- Create lookalike audiences from upsell accepters
- Optimize ad campaigns for higher-value customers

### Google Analytics

Track upsell performance in Google Analytics (GA4 and Universal Analytics).

**Events Tracked**:
- `view_item`: Offer impression
- `add_to_cart`: Offer acceptance
- `purchase`: Upsell revenue

**Setup**:
1. Configure Google Analytics in Shopify
2. Enable Enhanced Ecommerce tracking
3. ReSell sends events automatically

**View in GA4**:
- Navigate to **Events** in GA4
- Filter by ReSell events
- Analyze conversion funnels

### Google Ads Conversion Tracking

Track upsell conversions for Google Ads campaigns.

**Setup**:
1. Create conversion action in Google Ads
2. Install Google Ads conversion tag in Shopify
3. ReSell tracks upsell conversions separately

**Benefits**:
- Attribute upsell revenue to ad campaigns
- Calculate true customer lifetime value
- Optimize bidding for higher AOV customers

## A/B Testing with Analytics

Use analytics to test different strategies:

## Optimization Tips

Use analytics to continuously improve performance:

### High Impressions, Low Conversions

**Problem**: Many customers see offers but few accept

**Solutions**:
- Increase discount percentage
- Choose more relevant products
- Improve offer design and copy
- Test different product types (upsell vs cross-sell)

### Low Impressions

**Problem**: Offers aren't showing to many customers

**Solutions**:
- Broaden trigger conditions (more products or collections)
- Check payment method compatibility
- Verify funnel is published and active
- Review display conditions (location, cart value, etc.)

### Good Overall Performance, Specific Offers Underperforming

**Problem**: Some offers in funnel convert well, others don't

**Solutions**:
- Replace low-performing products
- Adjust discount on weak offers
- Change offer sequence (move best performers first)
- Update product images or descriptions

### Declining Performance Over Time

**Problem**: Conversion rates dropping gradually

**Solutions**:
- Refresh product offerings seasonally
- Test new discount levels
- Update offer designs
- Rotate products to prevent offer fatigue
