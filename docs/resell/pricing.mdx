ReSell offers straightforward pricing with no revenue sharing or transaction fees. Choose the plan that fits your business needs.

## Pricing Plans

<Note>
ReSell does not charge any commission on sales generated through post-purchase offers. All additional revenue goes directly to you.
</Note>

### Free Plan

Perfect for getting started and testing ReSell.

**Features**:
- Unlimited post-purchase offers
- Unlimited funnels
- Basic analytics
- Email support
- All core features included

**Limitations**:
- Limited to 50 accepted offers per month

**Price**: **$0/month**

### Pro Plan

For growing stores looking to scale their upsell strategy.

**Everything in Free, plus**:
- Unlimited accepted offers
- Advanced analytics & reporting
- Priority email support
- Custom offer templates
- A/B testing capabilities
- Checkout upsells (Shopify Plus)

**Price**: **$29.99/month**

### Enterprise Plan

Custom solutions for high-volume merchants.

**Everything in Pro, plus**:
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom funnel development
- White-glove onboarding
- Advanced integrations
- Custom reporting

**Price**: **Contact us for pricing**

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are there any additional payments in ReSell?">
    No. ReSell has a simple monthly subscription with no hidden fees, commission, or transaction charges. The price you see is the price you pay.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed 50 offers on the Free plan?">
    Once you reach 50 accepted offers in a month, funnels will pause until the next billing cycle or you upgrade to Pro. Your existing funnels and data remain intact.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?">
    Yes! You can change plans at any time from your ReSell dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If ReSell doesn't meet your needs, contact support@devit.software for a full refund.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is Shopify Plus required?">
    No. ReSell works with all Shopify plans (Basic, Shopify, Advanced, and Plus). However, checkout upsells are only available for Shopify Plus merchants.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
