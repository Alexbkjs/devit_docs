## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are label rules and badge rules?">
    A **label rule** is a configured label with its triggers, conditions, and display settings. A **badge rule** is the same but for badges. Each rule can apply to multiple products based on your conditions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?">
    Yes! You can change plans at any time from the Lably dashboard or Shopify admin. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to my labels if I downgrade?">
    If you downgrade to a plan with fewer rules, labels exceeding the limit will be paused but not deleted. You can reactivate them if you upgrade again or decide which ones to keep active.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you offer a trial period?">
    Yes! All paid plans include a 7-day free trial. The Free plan is available permanently with 1 label and 1 badge rule.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How much do I save with yearly billing?">
    You save **20%** on all paid plans when you choose yearly billing:
    - Basic: $57.50/year (vs $71.88/year monthly)
    - Grow: $76.70/year (vs $95.88/year monthly)
    - Plus: $143.90/year (vs $179.88/year monthly)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are there any additional costs?">
    No. The subscription is the only cost. There are no hidden fees, per-label charges, or transaction fees.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    All payments are processed through Shopify, so you can use any payment method accepted by Shopify.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
