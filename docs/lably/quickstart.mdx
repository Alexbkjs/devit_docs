This guide will walk you through creating your first label or badge in Lably. You'll learn the basics of customization and how to apply labels to your products.

## Prerequisites

- Lably app installed from the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/lably)
- Access to your Shopify store admin panel

## Create Your First Label

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Lably App">
    From your Shopify admin, navigate to Apps and click on Lably to open the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add New Label">
    Click the **Add new** button and select **Add new label** from the options.

    <Note>
    **Labels** display on product images, while **Badges** display in product content areas.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Label Type">
    Select your label type:
    - **Image**: Upload your own image or choose from pre-designed templates
    - **Text**: Create a text-based label with full style customization
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize Your Label">
    Use the left-side menu to customize all aspects of your label. See [Customization Options](#customization-options) below.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save & Activate">
    Click **Save** to activate your label. It will immediately appear on products based on your display conditions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customization Options

### Label/Badge Content

Choose between two content types:

**Image Labels**
- Upload custom images
- Select from pre-designed template library
- Support for PNG, JPG, SVG formats

**Text Labels**
- Enter custom text
- Add translations for multiple languages
- Dynamic text options

<Tip>
You can add translations to your labels by clicking **Locales** next to the text field and selecting your target languages.
</Tip>

### Styles

Customize the visual appearance of your label:

- **Shape**: Rectangle, circle, rounded corners, custom shapes
- **Font**: Choose from Google Fonts or upload custom fonts
- **Size**: Adjust width, height, and text size
- **Colors**: Background, text, border, and shadow colors
- **Effects**: Borders, shadows, opacity, and gradients

### Position & Layout

Control where your label appears on product images:

- **Presets**: Top-left, top-right, bottom-left, bottom-right, center
- **Custom Position**: Fine-tune with margin controls
- **Responsive**: Automatically adapts to different screen sizes

### Animations

Add motion to draw attention:

- Pulse, bounce, shake, flash effects
- Control animation speed and timing
- Option to disable animations on mobile

### Links

Make labels clickable:

- Link to specific products
- Direct to collection pages
- Create custom URLs for landing pages

<Warning>
Ensure linked URLs are valid and accessible to avoid broken links on your storefront.
</Warning>

### Week Schedule

Control when labels appear throughout the week:

- Enable/disable for specific days
- Set different time ranges for each day
- All days enabled by default (24/7 display)

### Visibility on Pages

Choose where labels appear across your store:

- **Home Page**: Show on homepage product grids
- **Collection Pages**: Display in collection views
- **Product Pages**: Appear on individual product pages
- **Search Results**: Show in search result listings

### Display Period

Set automatic start and end dates:

- **All Time**: Label stays active until manually removed (default)
- **Custom Period**: Set specific start and end dates
- Perfect for limited-time promotions

### Display Conditions

Target specific products with advanced rules:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Product Conditions">
    - Product tags
    - Product type
    - Product vendor
    - Specific products
    - Product collections
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Inventory Conditions">
    - Stock quantity (low stock alerts)
    - In stock vs out of stock
    - Inventory policy
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Price Conditions">
    - Price range
    - Compare at price (on sale)
    - Discount percentage
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Customer & Location">
    - Visitor country/region
    - Customer tags
    - First-time vs returning visitors
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Example: Create a Sale Badge

Here's a quick example to create a "50% OFF" sale badge:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Badge">
    Click **Add new** → **Add new badge**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Text">
    Select **Text** type and enter "50% OFF"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Style It">
    - Choose red background color
    - Set white text color
    - Select bold font
    - Add rounded corners
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Conditions">
    Go to **Display Conditions** → **Add condition** → **Compare at price**

    This ensures the badge only shows on products with a compare-at price (on sale).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Schedule It">
    Set **Display Period** to match your sale dates
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** and the badge will appear on all sale products
  </Step>
</Steps>
