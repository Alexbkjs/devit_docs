Selecty offers a feature-rich free plan plus premium tiers for stores that need advanced customization and multi-store capabilities.

<Note>
All charges are billed in USD. Recurring and usage-based charges are billed every 30 days.
</Note>

## Pricing Plans

### Free Plan

Perfect for most stores and includes all essential features.

**Features**:
- Shopify Markets Integration
- Unlimited selectors for your store
- Unlimited page views
- Personalized recommendations banner
- 24/7 basic support

**Price**: **Free**

### Professional Plan

For stores needing advanced selector types and automatic redirects.

**Pricing**: **$11.99/month** or **$107.91/year** (save 25%)

**Features**:
- All from Free
- Pro selectors & recommendations types
- Automatic markets redirects
- 24/7 chat, email, and phone support
- 7-day free trial

### Premium Plan

For agencies and stores managing multiple storefronts.

**Pricing**: **$21.99/month** or **$197.91/year** (save 25%)

**Features**:
- All from Professional
- Titles and icons customization
- Connect multiple stores
- Custom theme styles
- 24/7 chat, email, and phone support
- 7-day free trial

## Choosing the Right Plan

Not sure which plan fits your needs? Here's our recommendation:

- **Free**: Most stores - includes all essential features for currency, language, and market switching
- **Professional**: Stores wanting automatic market redirects and premium support
- **Premium**: Agencies, brands with multiple stores, or stores needing complete customization control

<Tip>
Start with the Free plan to test Selecty. You can upgrade anytime if you need advanced features like automatic redirects or multi-store management.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is the free plan really unlimited?">
    Yes! The free plan has no limits on:
    - Number of selectors
    - Page views
    - Currencies, languages, or markets
    - Store traffic

    You can use Selecty free forever without any usage restrictions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are 'Pro selectors & recommendations types'?">
    Pro selector types include advanced dropdown designs, enhanced visual styles, and additional placement options that go beyond the standard selectors in the free plan. These provide more flexibility for matching your brand and improving user experience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is 'Automatic markets redirects'?">
    This feature automatically redirects visitors to their local Shopify Market based on their geolocation. For example, visitors from France are redirected to your French market, improving their shopping experience and increasing conversions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I connect multiple stores on Premium?">
    Yes! The Premium plan allows you to manage selectors for multiple Shopify stores from one dashboard. This is perfect for agencies managing client stores or brands with multiple storefronts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?">
    Yes! You can change plans at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated. Downgrading to Free retains your basic selectors but removes premium features.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much do I save with yearly billing?">
    You save **25%** on paid plans with yearly billing:
    - Professional: $107.91/year (vs $143.88/year monthly)
    - Premium: $197.91/year (vs $263.88/year monthly)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer a trial period?">
    Yes! Both Professional and Premium plans include a 7-day free trial. The Free plan is available permanently with no trial needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    All payments are processed through Shopify, so you can use any payment method accepted by Shopify.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
