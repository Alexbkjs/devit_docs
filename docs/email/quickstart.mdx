This guide will walk you through setting up Email Bot and creating your first email integration. You'll be forwarding emails to Discord in just a few minutes.

## Prerequisites

- Discord server where you have permission to add bots
- Administrator or appropriate role permissions

## Setup Steps

### Step 1: Invite Email Bot

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Bot Page">
    Go to the [Email Bot page on top.gg](https://top.gg/bot/1106165894859735070)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click the **Invite** button to add the bot to your server
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Server">
    Choose the Discord server where you want to add the bot
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize Permissions">
    Review and authorize the requested permissions

    <Note>
    Email Bot needs permissions to send messages, embed links, and attach files to forward emails properly.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Setup">
    Click **Authorize** to complete the bot installation
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Connect to a Channel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Target Channel">
    Navigate to the Discord channel where you want to receive emails
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use Connect Command">
    Type `/connect` in the message field and press Enter
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get Your Email Address">
    The bot will instantly create a **unique email address** for that channel

    The email address will look like: `channel-id@email.devit.software`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy the Email">
    Save this email address - you'll use it to subscribe to notifications or forward emails
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Each channel gets its own unique email address. You can connect the bot to multiple channels for different purposes.
</Tip>

### Step 3: Test the Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send a Test Email">
    From your personal email, send a test message to the bot's email address
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Discord">
    Within moments, the email should appear in your Discord channel
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Content">
    Confirm the email subject and any attachments were delivered correctly
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customize Display (Optional)

By default, emails show subject only. To enable content preview:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use /list Command">
    Type `/list` in the channel
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click **Settings** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Display">
    Choose your preferred options:
    - Email Preview in Message
    - Web Version of Emails
    - Email Personalization
  </Step>
</Steps>
